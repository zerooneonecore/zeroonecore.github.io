Tracing programs, maybe gdb


Anyway, so lets trace some syscalls with strace, lets trace gdb. First of all, running the command strace gdb will make gdb unuseable, it will essentially just completely take over standard output. The better way to go about it is strace --attach=<pid>, then you will be privy to the sea of output from strace. getting the pid from ps and grep is kind of annoying, maybe gdb could be kind enough to provide it.
